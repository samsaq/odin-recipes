Frites

Description

These are just fancy fries, ready for the average downtown bistro to mark up by at least 200%. The kind mentioned here are lightly double fried for fluffiness.

Ingredients

  • Potatoes (Preferably russet)
  • Salt (to taste)
  • Oil for deep frying
  • Any powdered seasoning you like

Steps

  1. Slice potatoes into batons
  2. Preheat oil to 350F
  3. Fry the batons until blond before removal
  4. Freeze for storage or otherwise cool the fries
  5. Fry the fries until golden brown and season while hot when the time comes to serve
